game of the year 2069

A Unity3D game created by Justin Chang (title WIP).

Click here to download the latest copy of GOTY2069: (Windows) (Mac)

Goal: Collect all 12 pick-ups in the least amount of time. You can collect pick-ups by shooting them or running into them.

Controls:

  • Looking: Mouse
  • Movement: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Shooting: Left mouse button or space
  • Pause (access mouse cursor): Escape or P

Notes: You can only shoot one bullet at a time (i.e. only one bullet can be on the screen at a time)
